Face Recognition and Attendance

Instructions for use:

1. Pressing 'Start' button on UI, the webcam will start capturing and show the video feed
2. Press 'c' to capture and create records from the video feed
3. If 'c' is pressed multiple times, multiple records will be made and added to record file
4. On pressing 'q' the video will stop capturing and the final record file is shown
